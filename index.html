About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate and graduate assistant in the Department of Computer and Information Technology at Purdue University, working under the supervision of Prof. Eric Matson. My research interests include machine learning and deep learning, with a particular focus on developing hierarchical deep learning models for time-series classification, particularly in applications related to wearable sensor-based human activity recognition.
